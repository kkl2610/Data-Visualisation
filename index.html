The Beauty Products Industry

The Beauty Industry is annually worth billions of US Dollars.

The following visualisation will provide an overview of the sector, trade values of each country and the distribution of beauty products types.

Industry Growth (2009-2019)

The Beauty Products Sector has seen a lot of growth, more than doubling its value within 10 years.

The region that exports the most beauty products is Europe ($28.1B USD).

The region that has seen the most growth is Asia from $3.9B USD in 2009 to $19.2B USD in 2019 which is nearly 5 times the amount.

This makes sense considering the countries that have the highest value in beauty product exports comes from Europe and Asia which we will see in the next section.

Country Trading

Trade balance refers to the difference between the value of a country's exports and the value of their imports.

  • A positive trade balance means the country exports more beauty products than it imports (net exporters).



  • A negative trade balance means the country imports more beauty products that it exports (net importers).

In 2020, the top net exporters of Beauty Products were France ($7.2B USD), Republic of South Korea ($5.2B USD) and Japan ($3.8B).

For total exports, France remains in first place, exporting a value of $8.9B USD. This is followed by Singapore ($6.2B USD) then Republic of South Korea ($6.1B USD).

In 2020, the top net importers of Beauty Products were China (-$15.2B USD), Hong Kong ($-3.1B USD) and Russia ($-7.6B USD).

For total imports, China remains in first place, importing a value of $17.3B USD. This is followed by Hong Kong ($7.2B) who remains in second with the United States ($4.5B USD) in third place.

Trade Balance Over the Years

When comparing the Trade balance of countries within the last four years, many countries remain as either net importers or net exporters of beauty products.

France has steadfastly remained a net exporting country and China has remained a net importing country.

Looking at India, they began as net exporters in 2017 ($25.4B USD), then become net importers in 2018 (-$20B USD) and 2019 (-$53.9B USD) before becoming net exporters again in 2020 ($3.6B USD).

Type of Beauty Products

In 2019, 77.19% of exported Beauty Products were Beauty, makeup and suntan preparation products worth $43.7B USD.

The second highest product making up 8.46% of exports were Eye make-up preparation products worth $4.79B USD.

The third highest product making up 7.46% of exports were Lip make-up preparation products worth $4.3B USD.
